Assessment of Awareness, Adherence, and Barriers to Device-Associated Infection Prevention Measures in Intensive Care Units: A Multi-Center Cross-Sectional Study Corresponding Author
American Journal of Infection Control  ·  Article in Press  ·  2026
Overview: Coordinated a multicenter cross-sectional study across 20 Iranian ICUs, leading the statistical analysis and data harmonization pipelines to assess adherence to DAI prevention guidelines (CLABSI, CAUTI, VAP). Association of Brain and Ventricular Boundary Shift Integral Measurements with CSF Biomarkers: A Case-Control Study
Current Alzheimer Research  ·  Published  ·  Jun 2025
Overview: Investigated the relationship between cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) biomarkers (Aβ1-42, p-tau181) and longitudinal brain volumetric changes using neuroimaging data from the ADNI cohort. The study utilized the Brain/Ventricular Boundary Shift Integral (BSI) to demonstrate that these biomarkers are significant statistical predictors of early brain atrophy in Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI), underscoring their utility in monitoring early-stage neurodegeneration.

Evaluation of Serum Ferritin Levels Based on the Type of Iron Chelation Therapy in Patients with Major Thalassemia Medical Doctorate Thesis
Tehran University of Medical Sciences  ·  Defended  ·  July 2025
Overview: A clinical cross-sectional analysis of 56 β-thalassemia major patients evaluating predictors of iron overload. Extracted and processed clinical EMR data, subsequently utilizing ordinal logistic regression to demonstrate that blood transfusion interval intensity and patient age are significant independent predictors of severe serum ferritin elevation, whereas the specific type of iron chelation therapy showed no statistically significant difference in efficacy.
